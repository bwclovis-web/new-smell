## Future Ideas

A forward-looking list of enhancements and product ideas to improve customer satisfaction, retention, and operational efficiency.

### Customer experience
- Personalized scent profiles with onboarding quiz and evolving taste model
- Smart recommendations based on notes, seasons, and community ratings
- Compare perfumes side-by-side (notes, longevity, sillage, reviews)
- “Because you liked…” suggestions with similarity score explanations
- Advanced filters (notes, house, price, availability, trade/swap, season)
- Saved searches and alerts for rare/limited releases

### Community and trust
- Trader reputation score (response time, successful trades, verified badges)
- Verified purchase/trade markers on reviews
- Review helpfulness voting and “top review” badges
- Dispute resolution workflow and audit trail
- User follow system for houses and traders

### Performance and reliability
- Route-level caching and stale-while-revalidate for high-traffic pages
- Optimize image delivery (dynamic sizes, AVIF/WebP conversion pipeline)
- Prefetch on hover for common navigation targets
- Progressive loading for review lists and long pages
- Background jobs for heavy tasks (emails, analytics, imports)

### Security and safety
- Optional 2FA for admins and high-trust traders
- Suspicious activity alerts and login anomaly detection
- Rate-limit tuning based on account trust level
- Content moderation queue with ML-assisted flagging

### Monetization and growth
- Premium memberships for early access or rare catalog drops
- Featured listings or boosted trades
- Affiliate partnerships with verified retailers
- Curated discovery collections and seasonal campaigns

### Operational excellence
- Admin dashboards for data quality (duplicates, missing notes, mismatches)
- Automated data validation and import reconciliation
- Error monitoring integration (Sentry/Datadog) with correlation IDs
- A/B testing framework for UX improvements

### Mobile and accessibility
- PWA enhancements (offline favorites, push alerts)
- WCAG-focused accessibility audit and continuous checks
- Gesture-optimized browsing and quick actions
