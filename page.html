    The Crush – Perfume Elixir by Fable Beauty
    Soft. Sweet. Completely Unforgettable.

    Meet The Crush—that fluttery, can’t-stop-smiling feeling when your favorite person walks into the room. She’s effortless charm, quiet confidence, and the kind of sweetness that feels natural, not forced. The girl next door with a secret smile and a heart full of what-ifs. Innocent, romantic, and just a little bit bold when it counts.

    The fragrance opens with Crisp Bergamot, bright and uplifting, like the rush of courage before saying hello. Water Lily blooms softly at the heart—clean, airy, and delicately floral, capturing that fresh, just-out-of-the-shower kind of beauty. It settles into Sugary Sandalwood, warm and comforting with a gentle sweetness that lingers close to the skin, inviting and intimate.

    Crafted in the USA and phthalate-free, The Crush is a 100ml perfume elixir made for daydreamers, romantics, and anyone who believes love often starts quietly.

    Scent Profile – The Crush
    • Crisp Bergamot – Bright, fresh, and lightly citrusy
    • Water Lily – Soft, clean floral with an airy feel
    • Sugary Sandalwood – Warm, smooth, and subtly sweet

    Key Benefits:
    • A soft, romantic fragrance perfect for everyday wear
    • Over 500 spritzes per bottle
    • Made in the USA with IFRA-compliant fragrance oils
    • Phthalate-free and skin-safe

    Usage:
    Spritz on pulse points—neck, wrists, or décolletage. Perfect for first dates, stolen glances, and moments when you want to feel effortlessly lovable.

    Ingredients:
    Denatured Alcohol, Fragrance Oil (Meets All IFRA Safety Standards)

    Net Weight:
    3.3 oz (100 ml)
